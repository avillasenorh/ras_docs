rapidAlignerSeismic Documentation

rapidAlignerSeismic is a Python package for aligning seismic waveforms based on their similarity. It is based on the NVIDIA library rapidAligner, a CUDA-accelerated library to align short time series snippets in an exceedingly long stream of time series.

For rapidAlignerSeismic the short series snippets (queries) will be earthquake templates, and the long stream (subject) will be the continuous seismic recording.
